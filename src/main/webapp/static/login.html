A New Hope for Sith Universities

It is a dark time for the Sith. Cadet, Jedi, and even Sith Lords are struggling to submit forms to be reimbursed for their extra-cirricular studies to help improve their worth to the Sith cause.

Fortunately, with this prototype of a website, the Sith workforce now successfully submit forms to be passed to a higher ranking Jedi Knight, Sith Lord, or even a Benefits Coordinator for approval. Finally, the dark side will be reimbursed for the hard work they do serving the Galactic Empire.
